# Example: New Product Idea

## Prompt

```text
before you build: I want to build an AI tool that turns long podcast episodes into short LinkedIn posts for founders.
```

## What The Skill Should Focus On

- Who exactly needs this.
- Whether the current alternative is already good enough.
- Whether founders do this often enough to pay.
- Whether distribution is realistic.
- Whether the product is a thin wrapper around a generic model.

## Good Output Shape

```markdown
## Quick Reality Check

What you want to build:
- An AI repurposing tool for founders who publish long podcast episodes.

Biggest risk:
- This may be a low-frequency content utility with many free or bundled alternatives.

Validate before building:
1. Find 10 founders who published at least 4 episodes in the last month.
2. Ask what they currently use to repurpose content and what they dislike.
3. Manually create 3 LinkedIn post sets and ask whether they would pay for the next batch.

Recommendation:
Validate first
```
